The Pokémon's Dragon-type hits deal additional damage equal to half the Pokémon's level.
精灵的龙系攻击会造成额外伤害，数值等于精灵等级的一半。
Moves that can flinch are 50% more likely. Deals 50% more damage to Pokémon with flinch immunity.
使目标畏缩的技能发动畏缩效果的几率提升50%。对免疫畏缩效果的精灵造成伤害提升50%。
Takes half damage from Dragon-, Fairy-, and Ghost-type attacks.
受到龙系、妖精系和幽灵系攻击的伤害减半。
Pokémon that swap in for this have their status effects healed, and take 30% less damage that turn.
替换上场的精灵会恢复其异常状态，并且在当回合受到的伤害减少30%。
Immune to Dark-type and Bug-type moves.
免疫黑暗系和虫系技能。
Immune to Fighting-type and Rock-type moves. Prevents Stealth Rock damage.
免疫格斗系和岩石系技能。不受隐形岩伤害。
Can't be numbed, waterlogged, trapped, or have its Speed lowered.
不会麻痹、浸水、陷入束缚状态，速度也不会降低。
Doubles Defense in rainstorm.
在暴雨天气中，防御翻倍。
Special moves have a 30% chance to waterlog targets.
特殊技能有30%的几率使目标浸水。
Changes the weather to ring eclipse, which heralds the total ring eclipse. Lingers for 3 turns after leaving.
将天气变为环形日食，预示着日全食的到来。离场后天气持续3回合。
Boosts Attack by 20% in hail, or 40% if also below half health.
在冰雹天气中，攻击提升20%，若同时生命值低于一半则提升40%。
Its attacks are critical against numbed targets.
对麻痹的目标发动攻击时必定暴击。
Boosts Speed by 50% in rainstorm. Doubles random added effect chance in rainstorm.
在暴雨天气中，速度提升50%。在暴雨天气中，随机附加效果的几率翻倍。
Twice as likely for moves to be critical. Restores HP for half the damage it does with critical hits.
技能暴击几率翻倍。暴击时恢复造成伤害一半的HP。
At the end of each round, lowers other waterlogged Pokémon's Attack and Sp. Atk by two steps.
在每回合结束时，降低其他处于浸水状态的精灵的攻击和特攻两级。
Boosts Attack by 30% in hail.
在冰雹天气中，攻击提升30%。
Takes 25% less damage in a hail.
在冰雹天气中，受到的伤害减少25%。
Physical attacks against the Pokémon have a 30% chance to waterlog the attacker.
对该精灵发动的物理攻击有30%的几率使攻击者浸水。
The Pokémon and its allies' moves are twice as likely to be critical.
该精灵和其队友的技能暴击几率翻倍。
Can't be afflicted with any non-volatile status condition.
不会陷入任何非挥发性异常状态。
Prevents stat steps from being lowered (except when self-inflicted). Immune to weather downsides.
防止属性等级下降（自身造成的除外）。免疫天气的负面效果。
Immune to Steel-type moves and restores 25% of max HP if hit by one.
免疫钢系技能，并且受到钢系技能攻击时会恢复最大HP的25%。
Steel-type attacks deal 50% more damage, and the Pokémon is protected from critical hits.
钢系攻击造成的伤害提升50%，并且该精灵受到的攻击不会暴击。
Prevents half the self-damage from recoil. Powers up moves that have recoil damage by 20%.
防止一半的自伤。提升具有自伤效果的技能的威力20%。
At the end of each turn, if in sunshine, each Pokémon not immune to sunshine has their defensive stats lowered by 1 step.
在每回合结束时，如果处于阳光天气，每个非免疫阳光的精灵的防御属性会下降1级。
Flinching moves always activate their random effects.
使目标畏缩的技能总会触发其随机效果。
Becomes energy charged upon entry, doubling the power of its next Electric-type attack.
入场时获得能量充电状态，其下一次电系攻击的威力翻倍。
By sacrificing a held item the Pokémon didn't start with, it can negate one attack against it.
通过牺牲一个精灵入场时未携带的物品，它可以抵消一次针对它的攻击。
Becomes energy charged when hit by an attack, doubling the power of its next Electric-type attack.
被攻击命中时获得能量充电状态，其下一次电系攻击的威力翻倍。
Prevents the foe from escaping during eclipse.
在日食期间，阻止敌方逃跑。
Boosts Speed by 50% in rainstorm. Can't miss during rainstorm.
在暴雨天气中，速度提升50%。在暴雨天气中，攻击必定命中。
Lays an electrical hazard around the opponent when its HP is lowered below half.
当其HP降至一半以下时，在对手周围布置一个电击陷阱。
Boosts the power of rampaging moves by 30%, and makes them physical or special based on which would be more effective.
将暴走类技能的威力提升30%，并根据哪种更有效将其变为物理或特殊技能。

精灵一开始是一只非常逼真的皮卡丘！
